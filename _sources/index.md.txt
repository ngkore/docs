# NgKore Docs

NgKore is an open-source community led by a group of passionate researchers, engineers, and professionals working to advance the future of 5G Advanced and 6G technologies. 

Our work spans a wide range of focus areas, including: 
- **O-RAN**
- **AI/ML** 
- **Blockchain** 
- **Cloud-Native Telecom** 
- **5G/5G-Advanced/6G Core**
- **Post-Quantum Cryptography** 
- **Non-Terrestrial Networks (NTN)**

*At the heart of NgKore is a commitment to openness and interoperability.*
